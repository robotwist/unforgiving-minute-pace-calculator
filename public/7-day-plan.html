Free 7‑Day Training Week

A balanced microcycle you can use immediately. For exact paces, use the GoldenPace calculator.

Day 1 – Recovery Run

Easy

30–45 minutes conversational pace. Finish with 6×10s relaxed strides.

Day 2 – Threshold Primer

Threshold

10 min easy + 3×6 min at comfortably hard with 2 min easy jog between + 10 min easy.

Day 3 – Easy + Mobility

Easy

35–50 minutes easy. Add 10–15 minutes mobility/core.

Day 4 – Intervals

Interval

10 min easy + 6×3 min strong with 2 min easy jog + 10 min easy.

Day 5 – Recovery or Cross‑Train

Recovery

25–40 minutes easy or 30–45 minutes bike/elliptical.

Day 6 – Long Run

Endurance

60–90 minutes easy to steady. Last 15 minutes slightly quicker if you feel good.

Day 7 – Off or Recovery

Recovery

Optional 20–30 minutes very easy + mobility.
